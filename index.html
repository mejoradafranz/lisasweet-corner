About Us

Welcome to
Lisa's Sweet Corner!

We are a local bakery based in Ocoee, Florida, serving the surrounding areas. We specialize in beautiful floral cupcakes, handcrafted to look like edible flower arrangements. Every order is made fresh with care and attention to detail, creating cupcakes that are as beautiful as they are delicious.

We believe every celebration deserves something special, and we're honored to be a part of your most meaningful moments with sweet creations made just for you.

Freshly made to order. Beautifully crafted. Made with love.
Made Fresh to Order

Cupcakes are baked 1–2 days before scheduled pick up or delivery and frosted 1 day before pick up or delivery.

Flavors

Vanilla, Chocolate, Red Velvet, Strawberry

Storage Instructions

1–2 days at room temperature in an airtight container.

3–4 days in the fridge in an airtight container to maintain freshness.

Serving Instructions

Do not consume cold. Our cupcakes and frosting are butter-based and are best enjoyed at room temperature for the best taste and texture.

How to Order

Add your desired boxes to the cart. For bouquet cupcakes, kindly include the color palette or theme of your event and 2 colors of choice for boxed cupcakes.

I only offer delivery for bouquet cupcakes that require setup at the event.

Kindly include a valid phone number so I can reach you on the day of pickup or delivery.

For pickup orders, a code will be provided for gate access since I live in a gated community.

What is Included in the Full Price of a Cupcake Bouquet

• Handmade cupcake bouquet, made just for your event

• Your choice of cupcake flavors

• Colors customized to match your theme or color palette

• Hand-piped buttercream flowers on every cupcake

• Bouquet dome, base, and all materials needed to create the arrangement

• Setup and bouquet assembly

Each bouquet is freshly made to order and designed to be both a beautiful centerpiece and a delicious treat.

Delivery & Fee

• Bouquet cupcakes need to be delivered and set up on the day of the event at your venue. Pick up is not an option.

• $1 per mile

Set Up Requirements

Please provide a 1 to 1½ hour window minimum for set up.

The bouquet should not be set up outdoors or in direct sunlight, especially during the summer or in hot weather.

Please keep it indoors or in a cool, shaded area at 25°C (77°F) or below to help prevent the buttercream from melting.

Our Cupcakes Cannot Be Shipped!

For quality and freshness, we do not ship cupcakes. All orders are for local pickup or bouquet delivery only.

Changes & Cancellations

Small orders may be changed or canceled up to 72 hours before the scheduled pickup or delivery date for a full refund. Big orders must be canceled at least 2 weeks in advance for a full refund.

Once an order is within 72 hours (small orders) or 2 weeks (big orders) of pickup or delivery, materials, ingredients and preparation will already be in progress. At that point, cancellations will receive a 50% refund only.

Disclaimers

All of our desserts are made in a home kitchen that may process common allergens, including milk, eggs, wheat, soy, peanuts, and tree nuts. While every effort is made to avoid cross-contamination, we cannot guarantee an allergen-free environment.

Design Differences

Each floral cupcake is handcrafted, so designs and flower arrangements may vary slightly.

Colors may also appear different due to lighting and screen settings.

By placing an order, you confirm that you have read and agree to the terms and conditions above.
